Tushar Prakash


I am a ML researcher at Sony Research India, working on personalization and optimization. Previously, I worked on domain adaptation with the STARS Research Team at Inria Centre at Université Côte d’Azur. I also interned at the University of Alberta, where I worked on adversarial robustness. I have spent a "bit" of my time at Microsoft as SDE intern, developing testbeds for 5G infrastructure. As an undergraduate student, I studied Electronics and Communication Engineering at the Delhi Technological University (India).

For any inquiries, feel free to reach out to me via mail!

CV Mail Twitter Scholar Github LinkedIn

Awards: “In 2018, I graduated at the top of my class from high school. I completed my undergraduate studies in 2023, graduating with First Division with Distinction. In 2022, I was selected for the Mitacs Global Research Internship (GRI) at the University of Alberta. In 2023, I was honored with the Best Paper Award for CR-SoRec at Sony’s annual conference for my contributions to research.”
